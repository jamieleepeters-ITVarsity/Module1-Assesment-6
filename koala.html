Baby koalas eat their mother’s fecal matter For as cute as they are, koalas do have some rather icky facts. One of them? After they have been weaned off milk, young koalas (known as joeys) will eat their mother’s fecal matter before they switch to eating leaves on their own. 1 While it may seem gross, koalas aren’t the only species to do this: pandas, hippos, and even elephants are known for doing the same. And there’s some science behind it.
